GRID CAMPAIGN

What this about

Make a difference with a click! By clicking on the ball below, you'll be making a donation to support the Bilingual Sports Academy, Juega Como Campeon (JCC), and positively impact the lives of disadvantaged children in the Dominican Republic. Each click represents an opportunity for these children to access sports programs, academic support, and character-building activities.

- We develop sports skills, provide academic support in English and Math, and instill values like responsibility and community service.
- At JCC, we use sports to empower children, offering them new opportunities and a promising future.
- Your contribution doesn't just buy a ball; it opens doors to a better tomorrow for these young individuals.

Join us and be part of the positive change in these children's lives in the Dominican Republic! Your donation makes a difference!

LET'S BUILD A HOOPS COURT !

By clicking on one of the balls below, you'll be making a donation in that amount to support Juega Como Campeon (JCC) and positively impact the lives of underprivileged Dominican youth to whom JCC engages in educational reinforcement (primarily English), athletic excellence, gratitude, community service, integrity and personal responsibility.

We have received final approval from the local sports ministry in Puerto Plata granting JCC permission to install fixed backboards with rims on their land. This will provide JCC with a more stable home for daily practice. We just need the added funds to proceed. So please consider spreading the word (and this link) to your groups and invite them to consider JCC to be a part of their philanthropy this Christmas season.

And if you feel inclined to give more than the corresponding amount, please write us at juegacomocampeon@gmail.com
